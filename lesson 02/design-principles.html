Rule of Thirds

The Rule of Thirds basically talks about dividing an area into threes for a more aesthetic environment.

White Space and Clean Design

White Space isn't about using what's IN the webpage, but rather what is missing from it. We don't need to overcrowd with graphics and text in order to get our point across.
