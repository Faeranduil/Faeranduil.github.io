This Weather Blows

Target Audience

The target audience for this site would probably be:

  • Any travelers who would be passing through or visiting these cities
  • The residents of these towns who need to know the weather on the daily
  • Possibly the weather stations (Sounds redundant I know, but you'd be surprised how many meteorologists actually just check weather apps or websites)

Personas

  1. Grandma Peterson is planning on visiting her grandkids during the Christmas Holiday. Unsure if she will need a heavy coat and snow chains on her tires, or just a simple jacket and some wool socks, she will need to check the weather.
  2. Billy Batson, when not saving the world as Shazam has decided to pursue a college degree. Just so happens that the college he is looking to go to is in one of these towns. He'll need this information if he is wanting to know if:
    • the winters are harsh and cold and barren
    • the summers are mild or burning hot
    • if he should even bother to pack skiis
